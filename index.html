Overview

Indoor Positioning Platform is a Wi-Fi / UWB based indoor localization system for smart environments. It collects RSSI and channel information from multiple access points and estimates positions on a live dashboard.

Data are uploaded via MQTT to a cloud database (AliCloud / AWS). A web dashboard visualizes trajectory, heatmap and accuracy metrics for experiments and demos.

ECG Signal

Electrocardiogram (ECG) is an essential indicator of the occurrence, transmission, and recovery of cardiac excitation. It provides important reference information for evaluating heart rate, rhythm, and cardiac function.

The three main components are: P wave (atrial depolarization), QRS complex (ventricular depolarization), and T wave (ventricular repolarization). These signals together reflect the electrical activity of the heart.

The ECG system captures and analyzes these signals in real time, enabling early detection of abnormalities such as arrhythmia, conduction block, or myocardial hypertrophy.
